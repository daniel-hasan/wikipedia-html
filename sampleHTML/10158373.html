{{NFL player}} John F. McBride (born November 30, 1901 in Conshohocken, Pennsylvania) was an American football player who played the positions of halfback, fullback, and quarterback in the National Football League (NFL). McBride played collegiately at Syracuse University where he finished second in the nation in scoring in his senior year to Heinie Benkert.[1] McBride scored 90 points on 7 touchdowns, 11 field goals, and 15 extra points[1] in his senior year.
