Penny-Farthing Press is a comic book publishing company located in Houston, Texas, in the United States. Started in 1998 with "a plan to create comic books and children's books that exemplified quality storytelling, artwork, and printing," Penny-Farthing has expanded from its single original title, The Victorian, to six comic series, including Captain Gravity, Decoy, The Loch, Para, and Zendra.
