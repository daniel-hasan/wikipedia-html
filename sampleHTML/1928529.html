{{Infobox Ort in Deutschland}} Empfingen is a municipality in the district Freudenstadt in Baden-Württemberg, Germany.

Empfingen itself comprises two local districts, Wiesenstetten and Dommelsberg. Empfingen is close to the federal motorway 81 Stuttgart – Singen (Hohentwiel) (A 81).

Empfingen has a size of 18.29 km² and 4,090 inhabitants. It is twinned with La Roche-Blanche, France.
