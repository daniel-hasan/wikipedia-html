Tulad Ng Dati (Filipino: "Just Like Before") is the thirteenth album by The Dawn, released in 2006. It is composed of two CD's; one an audio CD and the other a mixed-media CD containing music videos of various The Dawn songs, including vintage footage of the late Teddy Diaz doing a guitar solo, and acoustic versions of newer material.
