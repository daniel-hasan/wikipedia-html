Gaston Pauls (b. January 17, 1972 in Buenos Aires) is an Argentine actor.[1]

He works in the cinema of Argentina.

His first appearance as an actor was in an Argentine teenage TV series called "Montaña Rusa". After "Montaña Rusa" he left TV to make more serious film projects. His most prominent role as a film actor includes Nueve Reinas and Iluminados por el fuego.
