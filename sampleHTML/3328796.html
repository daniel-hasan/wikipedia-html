"Jingle Bells"/"U Can't Touch This" is a 2005 (see 2005 in music) Crazy Frog cover of the Christmas song "Jingle Bells" and a cover of "U Can't Touch This", (originally MC Hammer). In Belgium, Holland, Australia, New Zealand, Sweden and France, "Last Christmas" instead of "U Can't Touch This" was added as B-side.

Chart performances

In French SNEP Singles Chart, the single debuted at number 84 on December 3, 2005 and jumped to number 9 the following week. It was number 7, then number 5, which was its peak position with 45,680 weelky sales[1]. The weeks after, it kept on dropping in the chart. Its 2005 sales in France were estimated to 84,367[2].
