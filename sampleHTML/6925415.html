{{Infobox Album }} Chef Aid: The South Park Album is an album based on the Chef Aid episode of South Park.

The album was available with 2 collectible covers and in three different versions — "Clean", "Explicit", and "Extreme". The explicit version contained profanities (including "fuck") and carried a Parental Advisory sticker, but it was still censored, most notably on the tracks "Nowhere to Run," "Will They Die 4 You," "Bubblegoose," "Wake Up Wendy" (specifically the tail end of it — which is the intro to "Horny"), "Horny", and "Mentally Dull," even censoring lesser profanities such as "shit". The Extreme version was completely uncensored.
