Lijordet is a station on Røabanen of the Oslo T-bane system between Østerås and Eiksmarka, 9.6 km from Stortinget. It is the second of three stations on Røabanen within the Bærum municipality, and passengers travelling here from Oslo need to pay a higher fare.

The station was opened on December 22 1948 when Røabanen was expanded from Røa to Lijordet[1].
