Mac McWhorter is the offensive line coach for the Texas Longhorns and was the interim head coach for Georgia Tech's football team in 2001,[1] and only coached for one game, the Seattle Bowl against No. 11-ranked Stanford. Tech won, 24 to 14,[2] technically giving McWhorter the highest win percentage of any Georgia Tech football coach.[1] McWhorter is currently in his sixth year with the Longhorns.[3]
