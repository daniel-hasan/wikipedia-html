Gunnar Eriksson (September 13 1921 – July 8, 1982) was a Swedish cross-country skier who competed in the 1940's. He won the 4 x 10 km gold and the 18 km bronze at the 1948 Winter Olympics in St. Moritz

Eriksson also won the 50 km event at the 1950 FIS Nordic World Ski Championships in Lake Placid, New York.
