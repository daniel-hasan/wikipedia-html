Princess Anne may refer to more than one person:

  • Anne, Princess Royal (born 15 August 1950), daughter of Elizabeth II of the United Kingdom
  • Anne, Princess Royal and Princess of Orange (1709‑1759), daughter of George II of Great Britain
  • Queen Anne of Great Britain (1665-1714), was known as Princess Anne before her marriage with Prince George of Denmark.
  • Anne (1637‑1640), daughter of Charles I of England

Princess Anne may refer to more than one place:

  • Princess Anne, Maryland, USA
  • Virginia Beach, Virginia, USA, formerly known as Princess Anne County before incorporating as a city. A neighborhood in Virginia Beach is still named Princess Anne.

Princess Anne may also refer to Princess Anne High School in Virginia Beach, VA.

Princess Anne was also the name of 46202 Princess Anne, a steam locomotive.
