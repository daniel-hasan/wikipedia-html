Gogita Gogua (born October 4, 1983) is a Georgian footballer who currently plays for FC Saturn Moscow Oblast and is also a member of the Georgia national football team. While he mainly plays midfield, he can also play as a defender. Gogua has previously played for FC Tbilisi, FC Khimki and Spartak Nalchik.
