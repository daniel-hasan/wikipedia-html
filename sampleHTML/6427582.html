{{VictorianClosedRailwayStation}} Dalmore was a railway station on the South Gippsland line in South Gippsland, Victoria, Australia. The station was originally known as Koo Wee Rup West until around 1910, it operated until the late 1970s. All that remains of this station now is the platform mound, however the track is still in reasonable condition.
