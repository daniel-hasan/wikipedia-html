John Dorso
John Dorso (born c. 1944) is a North Dakota Republican Party politician who served as the North Dakota House Majority Leader from 1997 to 1999, and in the North Dakota House of Representatives from 1985 to 1999. He was also a candidate for the United States House of Representatives in 2000, against Dem-NPLer Earl Pomeroy.
