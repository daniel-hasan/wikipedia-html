Mohammed Salah ({{lang-ar}})[1] (January 1982) is an Egyptian former footballer who is the current head coach of Al-Shoalah.

Managerial Career

Haras El Hodood

Salah made them go to eight in the Egyptian Premier League of 2011.

Al-Shoalah

Salah was appointed manager of Al-Shoalah in June of 2011. He made a big impact after winning the Saudi First Division with Al-Shoalah in the 2011-12 season. He then lead Al-Shoalah to 3 wins and 4 losses in the 2012-13 Saudi Premier League season.[2]
