{{Infobox GAA ground}} Wexford Park is a Gaelic Athletic Association stadium in Wexford, Ireland. It is the home of Wexford GAA's football and hurling teams. After a recent development the ground has a capacity of about 25,000. It is located in the Clonard area on the outskirts of Wexford Town, and although the ground does not have floodlights it regularly hosts evening matches during the brighter Summer months.
