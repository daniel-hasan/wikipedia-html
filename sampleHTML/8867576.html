Upland Unified School District is a school district located in San Bernardino County, California that serves the community of Upland, California. It has a total population of approximately 63,800 and covers a total area of 24 square miles.[1]

History

Upland High School has been a part of the district since it left the Chaffey Joint Union High School District in 1991.

Schools

The Upland Unified School District is a K-12 district. It has ten elementary schools, two junior high schools, and two high schools. One of the high schools is a continuation school. The district enrolled 12,285 students in 2005. All of the schools are located in Upland, California.[2]
