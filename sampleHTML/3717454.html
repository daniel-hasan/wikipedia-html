{{Unreferenced}} {{Infobox Album}} Timmy the Turtle is a 7" single by NOFX. The title track was taken from the sessions for So Long and Thanks for All the Shoes. Duncan from Snuff sings the lead vocal on the song. This record was limited to 9,499 copies on green vinyl.

Track listing

  1. "Timmy the Turtle"
  2. "The Plan"
