{{Taxobox}} The Mountain Parakeet (Psilopsiagon aurifrons) is a species of parrot in the Psittacidae family. It is found in Argentina, Bolivia, Chile, and Peru. Its natural habitat is subtropical or tropical high-altitude shrubland.
