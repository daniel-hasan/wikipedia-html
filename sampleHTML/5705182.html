Damien Dante Wayans is an American actor, screenwriter, and producer. Born in New York City, he is the nephew of the Wayans Brothers, which consists of Keenen Ivory Wayans, Damon Wayans, Kim Wayans, Elvira Wayans, Shawn Wayans, and Marlon Wayans. He is the son of their sibling Nadia Wayans.

Damien began his career in 1987 as an extra in Eddie Murphy's concert film Eddie Murphy Raw. He acted with his uncle Damon Wayans in Major Payne Then he went on to writing and directing. His big break in acting was in Jamie Kennedy's film Malibu's Most Wanted when he played the role of Tec. Damien has written and directed episodes of his uncle Damon's sitcom My Wife And Kids. Damien is now preparing with his cousin Craig Wayans to produce and direct Damon's The Underground, a sketch comedy show on Showtime.
