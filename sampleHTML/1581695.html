Scottish Cup

Rangers won the Scottish Cup after a 5-1 win over Dumbarton.

Scottish Junior Cup

Strathclyde Juniors won the Scottish Junior Cup after a 2-0 win over Dunfermline Juniors in a replayed match, after the first was protested.
