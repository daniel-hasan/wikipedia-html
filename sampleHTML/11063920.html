Yasin Çakmak (born January 6, 1985 in Rize, Turkey) is a Turkish football player. He currently plays central defender for Fenerbahçe in the Turkcell Super League.

Yasin was called up to the Turkey national football team for EURO 2008 qualifiers in March 2007.[1] His first cap came on April 12, 2006 against Azerbaijan.[2]

On August 3, 2007, Yasin completed his move to Fenerbahçe S.K.[3][4]
