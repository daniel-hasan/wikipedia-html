Mel Stuart (born 2 September 1928) is an American film director and producer.

Stuart directed the fantasy-musical Willy Wonka and the Chocolate Factory (1971). He has directed a few other features, including If It's Tuesday, This Must Be Belgium (1968), and Running on the Sun: The Badwater 135 (2000).

Stuart also directed documentaries including Four Days in November and Wattstax.
