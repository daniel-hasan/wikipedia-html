right M-169 was a state highway that served as a connector route between U.S. Route 27|US 27 and M-55 (Michigan highway)|M-55 near Houghton Lake in Roscommon County.;; At the time, US 27 traveled around Houghton Lake to the east. M-169 was replaced by M-55 when the route of M-55 through Houghton Lake Heights was designated as M-55 Business (Houghton Lake Heights)|BUS M-55.
