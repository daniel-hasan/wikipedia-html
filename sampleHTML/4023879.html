Franck Perera (Born 21 June, 1984, Montpellier, France) is a race car driver. In 2006 Perera drove in the GP2 Series for the DAMS team, after four years of racing for the Prema team in junior championships. In 2007 he will drive for Condor Motorsports in the Champ Car Atlantic Championship http://www.speedtv.com/articles/auto/champcar/36010/.
