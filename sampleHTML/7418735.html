Aleksander Hall (b. 1953) was a Polish conservative politician. Activist of Movement for Defense of Human and Civic Rights, later a politician and member of Akcja Wyborcza Solidarność. In 2001, he quit politics to focus on research. Author of many books and articles on history, patriotism, etc. He is married to Katarzyna Hall.
