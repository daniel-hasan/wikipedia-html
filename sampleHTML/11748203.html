{{Football player infobox}} Ali Ashfaq (born on September 6, 1985) is a Maldivian footballer who plays as a forward for New Radiant. He is a member of the Maldives national football team.On October 2007,he moved to DPMM FC in Brunei. He moved to DPMM FC together with Ibrahim Fazeel.
