Stojan Protić (Serbian Cyrillic: Стојан Протић) (January 28 1857 - October 28 1923) was a Yugoslavian political figure. He served as the prime minister of the Kingdom of Serbs, Croats and Slovenes between 1918 and 1919, and again in 1920.
