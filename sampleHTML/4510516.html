{{Politics of the Isle of Man}}

Hazel Hannan; former MHK was the Deputy Speaker of the House of Keys and a former Education and DAFF Minister of the Isle of Man Government. She was elected as a Member of the House of Keys in 1986 for Peel as an independent after a failed attempt 5 years earlier standing for Mec Vannin. She is the President of Peel AFC.

She was defeated on 23rd November in the 2006 general election by Tim Crookall MHK.

Governmental positions

  • Minister of Education, 1991-1995
  • Minister of Agriculture, Fisheries and Forestry, 1995-1999
