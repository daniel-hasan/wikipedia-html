{{Infobox Film}} Bhakta Prahlada is a telugu movie made in 1942 by Sobhanachal banners about a devotee Prahlada in Hindu mythology. This is the second movie of the same story with more advanced technicians.

Plot

See The Story of Narasimha for original story.

The movie dialog is based on the drama version used by Surabhi telugu drama troupe in those days.
