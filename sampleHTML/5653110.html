András Paróczai (born 11 May 1956) is a retired middle distance runner from Hungary. He won three medals at the European Indoor Championships.

Achievements

Year Tournament Venue Result Extra
1979 European Indoor Championships Vienna, Austria 3rd
1980 European Indoor Championships Sindelfingen, West Germany 2nd
1981 European Indoor Championships Grenoble, France 2nd
