{{lowercase}} Zugg's MUD Client (zMUD) is a MUD client developed by Zuggsoft. It was first developed in 1995, and was made into shareware in 1996. It supports several enhancements to standard telnet such as the MUD eXtension Protocol (MXP) and the MUD Compression Protocol (MCCP) and features advanced mapping capabilities.

Recently, a new version has been announced by Zugg, to be known as CMUD.
