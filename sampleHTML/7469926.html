The Hospital ({{zh-tpl}}), is a Taiwanese drama produced by the group Yang Ming Production. The series, with thirty-nine 60-minute episodes (including commericials) made its début on 15 August 2006 on China Television (CTV). Its scenario is an adaptation of the same book written by Taiwanese author Hou Wen Yong (侯文詠).
