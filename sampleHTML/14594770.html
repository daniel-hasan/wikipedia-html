{{Football player infobox}} Jeronimo Barrales (born January 28 1986 in Adrogué) is an Argentine footballer currently playing for Club Atlético Banfield.

Barrales made his first team debut in 2006, and has since gone on to establish himself as a key member of the first team squad.
