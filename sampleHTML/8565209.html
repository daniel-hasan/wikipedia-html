Sangokushi Online (三國志 Online) is Koei's third online Romance of the Three Kingdoms game for PC platform.

Koei announced the development of its latest MMORPG in mid-2006. Koei planned on releasing Sangokushi Online in Japan in the first quarter of 2007 followed by the rest of Asia [1]. However Koei set their open beta test to start on September 27, 2007.[2] Additionally, these plans do not reference a possible North American or European release even though the game is developed in a multilingual country. Although this follows in Koei's tradition for Asia-only releases such as Nobunaga's Ambition Online and Uncharted Waters Online.
