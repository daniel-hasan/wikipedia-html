{{Politics of Vanuatu}} The Greens/Verts is a green political party in Vanuatu. At the last legislative elections, 6 July 2004, the party won 3 out of 52 seats. Its most prominent member is Moana Carcasses Kalosil, the deputy leader of the opposition and a former cabinet minister.
