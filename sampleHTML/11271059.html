Lasnamäe is the largest district of Tallinn, both by area and population. Local housing is mostly represented by 5-16 stories high panel blocks of flats, built in the 1970-1990s. The majority of the population is Russian-speaking. Population 160,834 (01.09.2006).

Sport

Ajax Lasnamäe represents the district in Estonian Meistriliiga.
