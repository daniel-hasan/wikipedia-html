F.lli Masciaghi Bicycles is a large, state of the art manufacturer, located outside Milan, Italy, selling more than $45 million (US) worth of bicycles in 1997. The company began in 1967 as the proverbial garage business, teaming up with Giovanni Pellizzoli in 1990 to manufacture and sell the Fausto Coppi line of frames. They utilize Columbus steel and aluminum tubing for their frames. Category:Cycle manufacturers
