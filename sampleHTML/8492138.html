{{Infobox road}} New York State Route 11B is a state highway in northern New York, USA. NY 11B acts as a link between Franklin County in the east and St. Lawrence County in the west.

NY 11B provides a parallel east-west route to U.S. Route 11, but follows a more southerly path between Potsdam and Malone. The western terminus, in the Village of Potsdam, is at a junction with US 11. The eastern terminus, in the Village of Malone, is at a junction with US 11, New York State Route 30, and New York State Route 37.
