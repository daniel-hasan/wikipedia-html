{{Politics of Ukraine}} The Rus'-Ukrainian Union (Rus'ko-Ukrajins'kyj Sojuz (Rus')) is a political party in Ukraine. At the legislative elections, 26 March 2006, the party was part of the People's Opposition Bloc of Natalia Vitrenko.
