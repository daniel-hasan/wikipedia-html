{{Infobox Syria Governorate}} As Suwayda (also Sweida)(Arabic: السويداء) is one of Syria's 14 governorates. It has an area of 5,550 km². Its capital and major town is Sweida.

Geographically the governorate comprises almost all of Jabal el Druze, the eastern part of Lejah, all Es Safa hills, and a part of the arid eastern steppe of Harrat Ash Shamah.

Demographics and population

As Suwaydā governorate has approximately a population of about 304,000 inhabitants "a 2002 estimation".[1] the inhabitants of the governorate are Druze and Greek orthodox, there are also small Muslim minority.
