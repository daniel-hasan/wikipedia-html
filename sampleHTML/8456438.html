Ulrike Sarvari (born 22 June 1964 in Heidelberg) is a retired German sprinter who specialized in the 200 metres.

She finished fourth in in 4x100 m relay for West Germany at the 1987 World Championships in Athletics and the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul. Over 60 metres she finished sixth at the 1989 IAAF World Indoor Championships and won the 1990 European Indoor Championships in Athletics. At the 1990 EIC she also won the 200 metres.

At the 1990 European Championships in Athletics in Split she won a silver medal in 4x100 m relay for West Germany together with Gabi Lippe, Andrea Thomas and Silke Knoll.
