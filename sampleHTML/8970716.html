Andrea Consigli (born January 27 1987 in Milan) is an Italian football player. He is a goalkeeper for Atalanta B.C. of Serie A, on loan at Rimini Calcio F.C. for the 2007-2008 season.

Consigli received his first call up to the Italian U21's in August 2006.
