{{Infobox CityIT}} Vitulazio is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Caserta in the Italian region Campania, located about 35 km north of Naples and about 15 km northwest of Caserta. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 5,613 and an area of 22.7 km².[1]

Vitulazio borders the following municipalities: Bellona, Camigliano, Capua, Grazzanise, Pastorano, Pignataro Maggiore.
