{{Taxobox}} The Meek's Lorikeet (Charmosyna meeki) is a species of parrot in the Psittacidae family. It is found in Papua New Guinea and Solomon Islands. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests and subtropical or tropical moist montanes. It is threatened by habitat loss.
