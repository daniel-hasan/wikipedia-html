Szczecin-Golęcino is a municipal neighborhood of the city of Szczecin, Poland situated on the left bank of Oder river, north of the Szczecin Old Town and Middle Town.

Before 1945 when Stettin was a part of Germany, the German name of this suburb was Stettin-Frauendorf. Official site of Golęcino: www.golecin.pl
