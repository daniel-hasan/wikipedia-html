{{infobox record label}} Love Police Records is an independent record label based in Surry Hills, New South Wales. Originally started by Brian Taranto as a concept born from an ironic tshirt design, who was then joined by Andy Kent (You Am I).

The motto for Love Police Records is simple:

stop talkin crap, record it, package it, put it out... someone will like it.

Love Police Records are distributed by Reverberation in Australia and New Zealand.
