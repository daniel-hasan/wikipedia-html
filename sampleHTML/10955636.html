{{Chinese name}} {{Infobox Badminton player}} Ji Xinpeng (Chinese: 吉新鹏, born December 30, 1977 in Xiamen, Fujian) is a Chinese badminton player.

Career

{{MedalTop}} {{MedalSport}} {{MedalGold}} {{MedalBottom}} Ji won the gold medal in men's singles at the 2000 Summer Olympics, defeating Hendrawan in the final.
