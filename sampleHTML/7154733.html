{{Virginia state highways}} This is a partial list of secondary state highways in the U.S. state of Virginia. The numbers begin with 600 and can go into five digits in populous counties such as Fairfax County. Because of the sheer number of secondary routes, only certain major ones will be listed here.

For information about the creation and history of Virginia's Secondary Roads System, see article Byrd Road Act.
