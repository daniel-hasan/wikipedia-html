{{Taxobox}} Dypsis onilahensis is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family. It is found only in Madagascar. It is threatened by habitat loss.

Source

  • Dransfield, J. & Beentje, H.J. 1998. Dypsis onilahensis. 2006 IUCN Red List of Threatened Species. Downloaded on 20 July 2007.
