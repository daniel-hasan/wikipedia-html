Borský Mikuláš is a large village and municipality in Senica District in the Trnava Region of western Slovakia.

History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1394.

Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 198 metres and covers an area of 49.982km². It has a population of about 3,879 people.
