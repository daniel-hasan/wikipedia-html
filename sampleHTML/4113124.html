Vladeta Jerotić (Владета Јеротић) (born August 2, 1924 in Belgrade, Kingdom of Serbs, Croats and Slovenes) is a Serbian writer and psychiatrist.

In 2000, he was elected as a member of Serbian Academy of Sciences and Arts in the Department of Language and Literature.
