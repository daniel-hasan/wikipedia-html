{{Taxobox}} The Ghost Flower (Mohavea confertiflora) is a plant of the family Scrophulariaceae. It is a native of the Americas, growing in arid regions, semidesert conditions, below 1000 m. It flowers March to April.

The name Ghost flower may also refer to Monotropa uniflora.
