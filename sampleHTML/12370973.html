{{Taxobox}} The Lutz's Poison Frog (Epipedobates flavopictus) is a species of frog in the Dendrobatidae family. It is found in Bolivia and Brazil. Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests, subtropical or tropical moist montanes, moist savanna, subtropical or tropical moist shrubland, rivers, intermittent freshwater marshes, and rocky areas. It is threatened by habitat loss.
