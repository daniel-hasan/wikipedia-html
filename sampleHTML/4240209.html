Gau-Algesheim is a town in the district Mainz-Bingen, in Rhineland-Palatinate, Germany. It is situated near the river Rhine, approximatively 20 km west of Mainz.

Gau-Algesheim is the seat of the Verbandsgemeinde ("collective municipality") Gau-Algesheim.

History of Wine

In 1457, under Count Philipp of Katzenelnbogen-Diez, "Wine from the Gau" ist documented. [1].
