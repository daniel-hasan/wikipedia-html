Adrian Dix is a Canadian politician, currrently serving as MLA for Vancouver-Kingsway in British Columbia, Canada. He was elected to the Legislative Assembly in the 2005 election.

A native of Vancouver, Dix is a graduate of the University of British Columbia.

Dix is a member of the British Columbia New Democratic Party, and from 1996 to 1999 he served as chief of staff to Premier Glen Clark.

He was elected as an MLA on May 17, 2005. He currently serves as the Opposition Caucus Deputy House Leader and Opposition Critic for Health.
