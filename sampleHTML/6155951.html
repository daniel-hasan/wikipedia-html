Anime Festival Orlando (AFO) is an anime convention held at the Wyndham Orlando Resort in Orlando, Florida in early August. AFO has a variety of anime events and programming, guests, and many of the things to be expected an at anime convention.

History

2000

AFO began as a two day convention August 19th - 20th, 2000 , achieving an attedance of 450 people at the Ramada Gateway in Kissimmee, FL. Guests included Steve Bennett, Marshall Hash, and Wendee Lee. [1]

2001

Anime Festival Orlando 2 July 27th - July 29th 2001

2002

August 16th - 18th, 2002 While Anime Festival Orlando 3 was arguably its best year, the budget behind the convention nearly bankrupted the con.

Anime Festival Orlando 5 August 6th - 8th, 2003

2006

Anime Festival Orlando 2006 was held July 28-30 at the Wyndham Orlando Resort in Orlando, Florida. Guests were Lindsay Cibos, Aaron Dismuke, Quinton Flynn, Marc Handler, Jared Hodges, Vic Mognogna, Jeff Nimoy, Jack Povlitz, Chris Sabat, Doug Smith, Veronica Taylor, and Travis Willingham. [2]

2007

Anime Festival Orlando 2007 was held August 3-5 at the Wyndham Orlando Resort in Orlando, Florida. Guests were Chris Sabat, Lindsay Cibos, Colleen Clinkenbeard, Jason David Frank, Marc Handler, Jared Hodges, Doug Smith, Sonny Strait, and Travis Willingham.
