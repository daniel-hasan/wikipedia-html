{{Infobox Greek Dimos}} The Municipality of Messatida or Messatis is an administrative district in Greece. It includes the communities of Kríni, southern Demenika, Savalia, and parts of Thea and Kallithea. The population is over 10,000. It is home to about 6% of the Patras urban population. Its area is 80-100 km². Forests cover about 20% to 30% of the municipality, urban houses and buildings cover about 5%, and the rest remains pasture and rocky mountains (Omplos).
