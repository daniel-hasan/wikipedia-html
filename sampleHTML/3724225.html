Maureen Arthur (born: 15 April 1934 in San Jose, California) is an American actress. In 1967, she played Hedy LaRue in the movie: How to Succeed in Business (Without Really Trying). In the mid 1960s she recorded a pop tune on the Carlton record label titled "Don't Make The Angels Cry" and "What Does He Do With Her".
