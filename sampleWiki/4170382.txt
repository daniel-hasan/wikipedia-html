'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is a [[provincial park]] in [[British Columbia]], [[Canada]].

==History and conservation==
The park was established July 21, 1966.

The parks aims to protect a representative stand of [[Pacific Madrone|Arbutus tree]].

==Location==
Located 20 [[kilometre]]s north of [[Nanaimo, British Columbia]].

==Size==
22 hectares in size.
