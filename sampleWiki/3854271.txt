''' Ciaran Lyng''' (born [[24 July]][[1985]]) is an Irish inter-county senior footballer and hurler, playing for Wexford.

He began his soccer career in England, as a youth player for [[Preston North End F.C.|Preston North End]], but did not make an appearance for the [[Football League Championship|Championship]] club before he moved on a free transfer to Shrewsbury Town in December 2004.

He played for Shrewsbury on very few occasions - he made just four substitute appearances in the latter half of the [[2004-05 in English football|2004-05 season]].
In the [[2005-06 in English football|2005-06 season]] for Shrewsbury, he made just one start, in an [[LDV Vans Trophy]] match which Shrewsbury lost to [[Cheltenham Town F.C.|Cheltenham Town]].

After returning to Ireland he was selected to represent his county  on the senior football and hurling teams. He has represented Wexford in football All-Ireland and League competitions.
