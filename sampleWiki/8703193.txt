'''Bhakta Prahlada''' is a [[Telugu language|telugu]] movie made in 1942 by Sobhanachal banners about a devotee [[Prahlada]] in [[Hindu]] mythology. This is the second movie of the same story with more advanced technicians.

==Plot==
:See [[Narasimha#Scriptural_background|The Story of Narasimha]] for original story.

The movie dialog is based on the drama version used by Surabhi telugu drama troupe in those days.
