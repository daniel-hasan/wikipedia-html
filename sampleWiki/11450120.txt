'''Mohammed Salah''' ({{lang-ar|محمد صلاح}})<ref>{{cite web|url=http://translate.google.com/#en/ar/Mohammed%20Salah|title=Mohammed Salah Arabic Translation|publisher=Google Translate|accessdate=23 September 2012}}</ref> (January 1982) is an [[Egypt]]ian former [[association football|footballer]] who is the current [[coach (sport)|head coach]] of [[Al-Shoalah]].

==Managerial Career==
===Haras El Hodood===
Salah made them go to eight in the [[Egyptian Premier League]] of [[2010-11 Egyptian Premier League|2011]].
===Al-Shoalah===
Salah was appointed manager of [[Al-Shoalah]] in June of 2011. He made a big impact after winning the [[Saudi First Division]] with [[Al-Shoalah]] in the [[2011–12 Saudi First Division|2011-12]] season. He then lead [[Al-Shoalah]] to 3 wins and 4 losses in the [[2012–13 Saudi Professional League|2012-13]] [[Saudi Premier League]] season.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.soccerpunter.com/coaches/222694-Mohammed-Salah|title=Mohammed Salah Profile|publisher=soccerpunter.com|accessdate=23 September 2012}}</ref>
