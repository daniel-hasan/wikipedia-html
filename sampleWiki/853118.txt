'''Brunswick Street Station''' is an undercover [[railway station]] in the [[Queensland Rail|QR]] [[Citytrain]] rail network of [[Brisbane]], [[Queensland]], [[Australia]].

Brunswick Street Station, which is located beneath the [[Valley Metro (Fortitude Valley)|Valley Metro]] complex, is the station for [[Fortitude Valley, Queensland|Fortitude Valley]], and Brisbane's [[Chinatown]].

Pedestrian access to the station is via the corner of Brunswick Street and Wickham Street.

Nearby locations include Brisbane's Chinatown, the Brunswick Street Mall, and the [[Story Bridge, Brisbane|Story Bridge]].

The station has four platforms. It is located in Zone 1 of the [[TransLink (South East Queensland)|TransLink]] integrated public transport system.

==Services by platform==
 
Brunswick Street Station currently serves all suburban and interurban lines. The platforms at Brunswick Street Station are numbered from 1 to 4, with 1 being the easternmost platform and 4 being one of the westernmost. However during peak hour, some services may use different platforms from the ones listed below. The services which generally use each platform are listed below.

<b>Platforms 1</b> 
* <font color=#FF0000>[[Beenleigh railway line, Brisbane|'''Beenleigh Line''']]</font> - all stations and peak hour express services to Beenleigh
* <font color=#FEBE10>[[Gold Coast railway line, Queensland|'''Gold Coast Line''']]</font> - Interurban express services to Robina
* <font color=#800080>[[Cleveland railway line, Brisbane|'''Cleveland Line''']]</font> - all stations and peak hour express services to Cleveland
* <font color=#FF0000>[[Tennyson railway line, Brisbane|'''Corinda via South Brisbane Line''']]</font> - all stations to Corinda via South Brisbane and Tennyson (Peak hour only)
* <font color=dodgerblue>[[Roma Street railway station, Brisbane|'''Roma Street''']]</font> - Services terminating at Roma Street
* <font color=dodgerblue>[[South Bank railway station, Brisbane|'''South Bank''']]</font> - Services terminating at South Bank (weekends only)

<b>Platforms 2</b>
* <font color=#FF0000>[[Ferny Grove railway line, Brisbane|'''Ferny Grove Line''']]</font> - all stations and peak hour express services to Ferny Grove
* <font color=#FEBE10>[[AirTrain (Brisbane)|'''Airport Line''']]</font> - Airtrain service to Brisbane International and Domestic terminals.
* <font color=#009900>[[Nambour and Gympie North railway line, Queensland|'''Nambour and Gympie North Line''']]</font> - Interurban express services to Cooroy and Gympie North
* <font color=#800080>[[Doomben railway line, Brisbane|'''Doomben Line''']]</font> - all stations services to Doomben
* <font color=gray>[[Exhibition railway line, Brisbane|'''Exhibition Line''']]</font> - direct service to Exhibition '''(during special events only)'''
* <font color=dodgerblue>[[Bowen Hills railway station, Brisbane|'''Bowen Hills''']]</font> - Services terminating at Bowen Hills

<b>Platform 3</b>
* <font color=#009900>[[Ipswich railway line, Queensland|'''Ipswich Line''']]</font> - all stations and peak hour express services to Ipswich
* <font color=#009900>[[Rosewood railway line, Queensland|'''Rosewood Line''']]</font> - Peak hour interurban services to Rosewood (at other times, Rosewood line trains depart from [[Ipswich railway station, Brisbane|Ipswich]])
* <font color=dodgerblue>[[Roma Street railway station, Brisbane|'''Roma Street''']]</font> - Services terminating at Roma Street
* <font color=gray>[[Exhibition railway line, Brisbane|'''Exhibition Line''']]</font> - Services to Exhibition via Roma Street '''(during special events only)'''

<b>Platform 4</b>
* <font color=#009900>[[Caboolture railway line, Brisbane|'''Caboolture Line''']]</font> - all stations and peak hour express services to Caboolture
* <font color=#0000cd>[[Shorncliffe railway line, Brisbane|'''Shorncliffe Line''']]</font> - all stations and peak hour express services to Shorncliffe
* <font color=#009900>[[Nambour and Gympie North railway line, Queensland|'''Nambour and Gympie North Line''']]</font> - Interurban express services to Nambour (Additional off-peak bus and train services may depart from [[Caboolture railway station, Brisbane|Caboolture]])
* <font color=dodgerblue>[[Bowen Hills railway station, Brisbane|'''Bowen Hills''']]</font> - Services terminating at Bowen Hills
