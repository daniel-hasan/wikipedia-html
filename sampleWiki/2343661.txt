'''Meier Tzelniker''' ([[1894]]-[[1982]]) was a [[United Kingdom|British]] [[actor]].

He appeared mainly in [[Yiddish]] theatre, but was sometimes a character actor in English language plays and films, notably ''[[Expresso Bongo]]'' ([[1960]]).
