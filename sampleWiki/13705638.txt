'''Cristobal Lloréns''' was a [[Spain|Spanish]] painter, active during the late-[[Renaissance]] period. He lived in [[Valencia]] about 1597. He painted a history of ''St. Mary Magdalene and St. Sebastian'' (now lost) for the conventual church of San Miguel de his Reyes.
