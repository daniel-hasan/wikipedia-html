The '''[[Scouting|Scout]] and [[Girl Guides|Guide]] movement in Portugal''' is served by several organizations:
* [[Federação Escotista de Portugal]], member of the [[World Organization of the Scout Movement]], a federation of:
** [[Associação dos Escoteiros de Portugal]]
** [[Corpo Nacional de Escutas - Escutismo Católico Português]]
* [[Associação Guias de Portugal]] (AGP, ''Guide Association of Portugal''), [[World Association of Girl Guides and Girl Scouts|WAGGGS]]-member
* ''Associação das Guias e Escuteiros da Europa'' (''Guide and Scout Association of Europe''), [[Union Internationale des Guides et Scouts d'Europe|UIGSE]]-member
* ''Corpo De Escuteiros Maritimos'' (''[[Sea Scout]]s Corps''), [[World Federation of Independent Scouts|WFIS]]-member
* ''Fraternal de Antigos Escoteiros de Portugal'' (FAEP, '' Fraternity of Old Scouts of Portugal'')

==International Scouting units in Portugal==
The [[Transatlantic Council]] of the [[Boy Scouts of America]] serves American Boy Scouts in [[Lisbon]] and on the [[Azores]].
