'''Mohammad Rafiqul Islam Khan''' (born [[November 7]], [[1977]], [[Rajshahi]]) is a Bangladeshi [[cricket]]er who played a Test and ODI match for [[Bangladesh national cricket team|Bangladesh]] in 2002. He plays domestic [[first-class cricket]] for Rajshahi Division.
